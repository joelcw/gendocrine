This pilot experiment has two parts. In the first part, you will be required to listen to a number of sounds a decide whether you think the sound begins with an s or an sh. You can advance to the next sound by clicking 1 or 2 on your keyboard.

In the second part, you will be asked to answer some questions about yourself.

The data that is collected will be anonymous as you will not have to provide your name. Data that is collected will be shared within the research team but will only be used to inform further experiments.
